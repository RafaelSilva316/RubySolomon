My name's Ruby and I'm excited to meet you!

I am a born and raised New York State native. I grew up in Saratoga Springs where my love for the outdoors and people began. I strongly believe in living life intentionally and that living the best possible life in worth working for.

I believe hiking liberates the soul and reconnects us with the outside world as well as with our own bodies
Life is so precious. I get such joy from growing my own veggies. Although I hate tomatoes. YUCK!
Art gives us a medium through which we can better express ourselves. I love creating pottery not only because of the final product, but also because of the process itself.
There is so much beauty around us if we only look for it.
